# GateSync

GateSync is a web-first border logistics control tower for enterprises that operate cross-border trucking, container movement, drivers, shipments, yards, and border-gate workflows.

It helps logistics teams coordinate operations, monitor trip progress, reduce manual follow-up, and maintain a reliable operational timeline across all parties involved in a shipment.

## Product focus

GateSync focuses on internal enterprise operations first.

The product is intended for:

- **Logistics companies** that manage truck fleets, drivers, border procedures, and delivery coordination.
- **Cargo owners** that need visibility into shipment movement and border progress.
- **Customs agents** that coordinate declarations, gate procedures, and operational updates.
- **Operations teams** that need a shared source of truth for trips, incidents, documents, and notifications.

## Core capabilities

- **Trip control tower**  
  Track each trip as an operational timeline built from events, checkpoints, driver updates, border-gate progress, yard activity, and exception handling.

- **Fleet and driver coordination**  
  Manage vehicles, drivers, assignments, trip participants, and operational responsibilities across organizations.

- **Shipment and customs visibility**  
  Connect shipments, customs declarations, documents, and trip activity into one operational view.

- **Border and yard workflow support**  
  Support workflows around gate queues, yard entry, yard exit, inspection, clearance, handoff, and delivery milestones.

- **Event-based operational history**  
  Maintain a reliable history of what happened, when it happened, who reported it, and which system or participant provided the update.

- **Integration-ready operations**  
  Normalize authorized partner data, official APIs, enterprise exports, driver updates, and manual operations into the same event timeline.

- **Notifications and exception handling**  
  Help teams react to delays, missing updates, document issues, queue changes, and operational risks.

## Operating principles

- **Enterprise-first**  
  GateSync prioritizes internal B2B logistics operations before marketplace or driver-cargo matching features.

- **Timeline-first**  
  Trips are treated as event timelines, not only as a single mutable status.

- **Authorized data sources**  
  GateSync is designed around official APIs, partner agreements, enterprise-authorized imports, and user-submitted updates.

- **Tenant-aware operations**  
  Each organization’s operational data must remain isolated and accessible only to authorized users.

- **Human-in-the-loop control**  
  Automation and integrations should support operators without blocking manual intervention when external systems fail.

## Vision

GateSync aims to become a trusted operating layer for cross-border logistics teams, giving enterprises a shared view of shipments, vehicles, drivers, border activity, yard movement, documents, and operational exceptions.

The long-term vision is to support safer, faster, and more transparent border logistics workflows while preserving enterprise control, compliance, and data ownership.
